CubeRover
Design the Next Generation Space Rover
NASA x Astrobotic x CMU

CubeRover design, build, and manufacture for a new space age. CubeRover aims to commercialize and democratize access to planetary surfaces through low cost, reliable, modular rovers for revolutionizing planetary exploration. I serve as the UX Designer/Researcher at CubeRover.

Spring 2019 Group Project - Present
            Teleoperation Team Role: User Experience Designer/ Researcher
            In collaboration with:   NASA,  Astrobotics, and  CMU
            My Role:  Interaction Design, UX Research, Prototyping, Logic Flow
            Keywords:  Space, User Research, Interface Design, Data Visualization

I am responsible for designing interfaces for the Telecommunication team which will help the CubeRover team to control the rover when it lands on the moon. My responsibility includes helping users comprehend a complex environment from limited information as well as collaboration tools that will help our operations team plan and execute the mission safely.

Happy to chat more if you have any questions!
