CubeRover

Design the Next Generation Rover for Space

CubeRover design, build, and manufacture for a new space age. CubeRover aims to commercialize and democratize access to planetary surfaces through low cost, reliable, modular rovers for revolutionizing planetary exploration. I serve as the UX Designer/Researcher at CubeRover.


Fall 2018 Group Project - Present
            Teleoperation Team Role: User Experience Designer/ Researcher
            In collaboration with:   NASA,  Astrobotics, and  CMU
            My Role:  Interaction Design, UX Research, Prototyping, Logic Flow
            Keywords:  Space, User Research, Interface Design, Data Visualization

I am responsible for designing all the software that will run on the ground to let CubeRover team control the rover when it is on the moon. My responsibility includes lots of software to do data visualization and other tasks to help users comprehend a complex environment from limited information as well as collaboration tools that will help our operations team plan and execute the mission safely.

Due to project constraints, I am not able to share more information. However, I am always happy to chat more if you have any questions!
